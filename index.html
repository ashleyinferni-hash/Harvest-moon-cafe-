About The Harvest Moon Cafe

Our Story

The Harvest Moon Cafe was founded on the idea that every neighborhood deserves a cozy, welcoming space with excellent coffee and nourishing food. We source sustainable, high-quality ingredients to bring the rich, comforting flavors of the harvest season to you, all year round. Whether you need a place to work or a spot to meet friends, our doors are always open.

Our unique aesthetic, combining earthy tones with accents of gold and bronze, creates an atmosphere of timeless warmth that feels like coming home.

Allergen & Dietary Information

Your safety and dietary needs are important to us.

  • **Allergens:** Please inform your server of any allergies. While we take precautions, we cannot guarantee a cross-contamination free environment.
  • **Vegan (V) & Vegetarian (VE):** Items are clearly marked on our menu. We offer various plant-based milk alternatives (oat, almond, soy) for all our coffee drinks.
  • **Special Requests:** We are happy to accommodate modifications where possible. Just ask!
